Admissions

Below are the eligibility criteria for admission into our diploma programs:

  • Passed 10th Standard (Madhyamik or equivalent) exam for Admission in 1st year.
  • Passed HS Science for Admission in 2nd year
  • Passed ITI for Admission in 2nd year
  • Entrance Exam: Candidates must qualify the Vocational exam with 60% Marks for Admission in 2nd year.
🏷️ Fees: As per Council norms.
